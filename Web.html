Introduction

Breaking Bad is an American crime drama series about Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher diagnosed with terminal lung cancer. To provide for his family, he partners with a former student, Jesse Pinkman, to manufacture and sell methamphetamine. The show follows their journey into the criminal underworld, with White eventually adopting the alias "Heisenberg".
